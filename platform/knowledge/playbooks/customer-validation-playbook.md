# Customer Validation Playbook

Knowledge Asset KA-004 · v1.0 (AWP-004) · Pairs with
docs/CUSTOMER_VALIDATION_SYSTEM.md (the program); this is the HOW for any
product, any project.

## Rules
1. **Problem interviews before demos.** A demo biases everything after it;
   the first 20 minutes are about their last real decision, its cost, and
   what they used. Only then show product, and then *watch silently*.
2. **Hypotheses carry kill criteria.** A pain hypothesis without a
   condition that would disprove it is marketing, not validation.
3. **Count behaviors, not compliments.** "Cool!" = zero. Returns next week
   unprompted, uses own data, gives a date, pays = signal.
4. **Instrument the journey funnel** (launch → first insight → own data →
   artifact exported → shared) and review drop-offs weekly; every hesitation
   observed in a session is a UX bug with a name.
5. **Trade value for evidence.** Early adopters get real concessions
   (lifetime discount, credit); we get case studies + reference data.
6. **One page per interview**, same template: context · exact quotes on
   pain · cost quantified · reaction moments · kill-criteria verdict ·
   next action. Filed in knowledge/, shared with the founder raw.

## Anti-patterns (each observed in real startups)
- Pitching during "interviews" and logging politeness as demand.
- Building for the loudest persona instead of the converting one.
- Validating with friends only; skipping people who can say no with money.
- Postponing validation until the product is "ready" — the AWP-004 loop
  (30 minutes to shareable evidence) IS the interview artifact; nothing
  more is needed to start.
